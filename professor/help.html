Help

This is the Helper Page.

If you want to create a new Survey:

Select course on the leftside of the menu, after that the course name is visible in the middle of the screen
Now click on "Create a survey for this course", choose your question set and click next, on the next Page select a timeslot.
After clicking on "Create Survey" you will find the survey overview. Here you can find your ongoing and completed surveys.
Choosing what survey by selecting it, this will lead you to the specific survey, here you can compare to other survey or decide to delete the survey.

If you want to view ongoing or already completed survey:

Select course on the left side and choose overview of all courses.
Here you can find your ongoing and completed surveys.
Choosing what survey by selecting it, this will lead you to the specific survey, here you can compare to other survey or decide to delete the survey.
